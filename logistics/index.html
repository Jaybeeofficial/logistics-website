FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What types of shipping services do you offer?
We provide domestic and international shipping, freight forwarding, express courier, warehousing, and supply chain solutions. We also handle special cargo such as perishable goods, fragile items, and high-value shipments.
How can I track my shipment?
You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email through our online portal.
What is your average delivery time?
Delivery times vary based on destination and shipping method, but most deliveries are completed within 3–7 business days.
Do you handle customs clearance for international shipments?
Yes, we provide full customs brokerage and documentation services to ensure smooth clearance through international borders.
What items are prohibited from shipping?
Prohibited items include hazardous materials, explosives, illegal substances, live animals, and other restricted goods per international shipping laws.
